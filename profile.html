MICHAEL/ZC

Michael found coding in -of all places, an English class. After watching his professor demonstrate a NLP robot which wrote Shakespeare Sonnets, Michael was inspired. A handful of computer science classes later and a couple years of twists and turns, Michael now works both nomadically and in New York City on software projects that spark that same curiousity in him.

Technology is still about charisma and affect for him. He's always on the hunt for something inspirational and fun.

When Michael is away from his keyboard, he's climbing in the mountains -or dancing like a dork

|| PHILOSOPHY

Software and Technology should be measured by its impact on people. I believe in good code, maintainability, consistency and testability. But the most important thing about software should be about its impact on the customers, and the processes which enrich the people who build it.
